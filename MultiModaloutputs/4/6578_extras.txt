Caption/Title: Equipment Time/State Stack Chart  

Text surrogate:  
Equipment total time is decomposed into nonscheduled time and operations time.  
Operations time is further decomposed into equipment downtime and equipment uptime.  
Equipment downtime is decomposed into unscheduled downtime and scheduled downtime.  
Equipment uptime is decomposed into engineering time, standby time, and productive time.  
Manufacturing time is defined as the sum of productive time and standby time.
